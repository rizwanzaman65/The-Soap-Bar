The Soap Bar

The Soap Bar is located on the Sunshine Coast, Queensland, Australia and produces boutique style handmade soaps and natural body care products.

Designed to nourish your body and soul; we use skin-loving oilseed butters, aromatherapy essential oils, natural clays and other additives designed to deeply cleanse and to help you maintain soft, supple and youthful skin.
